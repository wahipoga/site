The Minds Behind

Eduarda Victoria

The Creator

Better known by the pseudonyms Wahipogo or Wahi, she is the creative mind behind the Profane Bonds universe. In addition to conceptualizing the work, she acts as screenwriter, creative director, and project manager. She is also a programmer, responsible for developing and maintaining the official website.
