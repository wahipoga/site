## 2. ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE POLICY
### 2.1 Brand Position
Laços Profanos is a project **made by humans, for humans**. Our characters, stories, and artwork are **artistic, emotional creations, thoughtfully made with human care**. We reject the use of generative AI that:
- Trains models using our works without consent
- Imitates our unique human artistic style
- Devalues the manual and intentional creative process

### 2.2 Absolute AI Prohibitions
**It is STRICTLY PROHIBITED to use any form of AI with Laços Profanos content:**
- ❌ Training, fine-tuning, or feeding AI models with our art
- ❌ Generating images/videos/text using AI based on our content
- ❌ Using "style transfer" tools to imitate our art
- ❌ Any process involving generative AI with our materials

### 2.3 Why We Prohibit AI
- **We value human authenticity** in every stroke, writing, and creation
- **We protect our artists** whose work is devalued by AI models
- **We maintain the genuine emotional connection** that only human art provides
- **We respect the creative process** that goes beyond simple algorithmic generation

### 2.4 Moral Rights:
Laços Profanos recognizes and defends:
- The artist's right to be recognized as the creator
- The right to have their style protected from algorithmic imitation
- The intrinsic value of the manual creative process
Any AI use that imitates our styles violates these moral rights.

### 2.5 Permitted Tools:
We allow tools that ASSIST but DO NOT REPLACE the human:
- ✅ Spell checkers
- ✅ Organization tools (Trello, Notion)
- ✅ Editing programs (Photoshop, Clip Studio)
- ❌ AI image/text generation
- ❌ Auto-completion based on our content

## 4. PROHIBITED Content and Narrative Exceptions
### 4.1 Absolute Prohibitions (Zero Tolerance)
**Never permitted, commercial or not:**
- **Any content generated by AI** using our materials
- Discriminatory ideologies (racism, homophobia, transphobia, etc.)
- Advocacy of real violence, abuse, or harassment **outside of a narrative context**
- Explicit sexual content (NSFW) with characters
- Promotion of illegal activities
- Political/religious use of the brand

### 4.2 Exception for Character Development
**Depiction of harmful acts is PERMITTED ONLY when:**
- It is part of a villain's or character's **narrative arc of redemption**
- The character **clearly learns** that such acts are wrong
- There are **narrative consequences** for negative actions
- It serves **critique, analysis, or character development**

### 4.3 Prohibition within the Exception
**Even within the above exception, it is STRICTLY PROHIBITED to:**
- Romanticize or glorify harmful acts
- Present harmful behaviors as desirable or positive
- Create narratives that justify harmful ideologies
- Fail to show the negative consequences of acts
