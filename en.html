About Us

Profane Bonds is a fictional story that was born as the idea for a manga inspired by popular works of the time. Over time, this first version was completely rethought, discarded, and rebuilt — and so the universe expanded until it became what we know today.

We are a completely independent project, developed and maintained by just two people. We work with dedication to bring this universe to life, create quality content, and share our vision with those who wish to know more about Profane Bonds.

Profane Tales

Profane Tales - Profane Bonds brings together various spin-offs set in the Profane Bonds universe. In the format of independent short stories, each episode presents a new story, with different characters, conflicts, and perspectives.

There is no chronological order or direct continuation between the chapters - each tale has its own identity and can be read separately.

Between battles, dramas, mysteries, and unexpected encounters, the work is born with the purpose of exploring all the creativity, depth, and possibilities that the "profane" universe has to offer.

Earth in Profane Bonds

In the Profane Bonds universe, planet Earth is not like ours. It is divided into only three continents, each with its own characteristics, cultures, and stories:

❄️ Kelster — the cold continent, marked by harsh climates, icy landscapes, and peoples accustomed to endurance.
🍃 Molton — the intermediate continent, with balance between cold and heat, natural and cultural diversity.
🔥 Clifton — the hot continent, full of intense heat, vibrant life, and traditions shaped by the sun.

The Creator

Better known by the pseudonyms Wahipogo or Wahi, she is the creative mind behind the Profane Bonds universe.
